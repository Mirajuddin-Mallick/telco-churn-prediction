Customer Churn Prediction

What is Customer Churn?

Customer churn refers to the percentage of customers who stop using a service or product within a given period. Understanding churn helps businesses take action to retain valuable users and improve satisfaction.

Why Predict Churn?

Predicting churn helps companies act before losing customers. With machine learning, we can analyze historical patterns and forecast future behavior. This reduces costs and boosts loyalty.

How It Works

We use data preprocessing, feature engineering, and machine learning models like Random Forest to analyze customer behavior and predict churn risk.
